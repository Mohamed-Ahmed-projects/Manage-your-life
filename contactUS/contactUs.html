General Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

Our website helps users set goals, manage to-do lists, track daily achievements, and stay organized using an integrated calendar.

Is this website free to use?

Yes, our basic features are free to use. We also offer premium plans with additional features. Please check our pricing page for more details.

Do you offer mobile app versions of the website?

No, curently we have no mobile apps but it come soon.

Account Management

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and submit the form.

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, go to the "Account Settings" page, where you can edit your name, email, password, and other personal details.

Goal Setting

How can I set a new goal?

Go to the "Goals" page, click on the "Add New Goal" button, enter your goal and the time needed to achieve it, and save it.

Can I edit or delete a goal after I've created it?

Yes, you can edit or delete your goals by going to the "Goals" page, selecting the goal you want to modify, and choosing the appropriate action.

Can I set recurring goals?

Yes, you can set recurring goals by selecting the "Repeat" option when creating or editing a goal, and choosing the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly).

To-Do List Management

How do I add tasks to my to-do list?

Navigate to the "To-Do List" page, click on the "Add Task" button, enter the task details, and save it. Tasks can also be linked to your goals.

How do I mark a task as completed?

In the "To-Do List" page, check the box next to the task you have completed. This will move the task to the completed section.

How do I prioritize tasks on my to-do list?

You can prioritize tasks by dragging and dropping them in the desired order or by assigning priority levels (e.g., high, medium, low) when creating or editing tasks.

Calendar Functionality

How can I view my tasks and events on the calendar?

On the "Calendar" page, you will see your current day’s events, tasks from the to-do list, deadlines for your goals, and any achievements recorded.

Can I sync the calendar with my Google Calendar or other calendar apps?

Yes, you can sync our calendar with external calendar apps. Go to the "Settings" page and follow the instructions under the "Calendar Sync" section.

Can I set reminders for events and tasks?

Yes, you can set reminders by selecting the reminder option when adding events or tasks to your calendar and specifying the time you want to be notified.

Achievements Tracking

How do I track my achievements?

On the "Calendar" page, you can select a day and add your achievements. Click on the "Add Achievement" button, enter the details, and save it.

How do I view my past achievements?

On the "Calendar" page, navigate to any past date to see the achievements recorded on that day.

Can I share my achievements on social media?

Yes, you can share your achievements on social media directly from the "Achievements" section by clicking on the share button and choosing your preferred platform.

Technical Issues

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you experience any technical issues, please contact our support team via the "Contact" page or email us at support@snap.com.

The website is not loading properly. What should I do?

First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser’s cache. If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance.

What browsers are supported by your website?

Our website supports the latest versions of major browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your browser up to date.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Can I delete my account and all my data?

Yes, you can delete your account by going to the "Account Settings" page and selecting the "Delete Account" option. Please note that this action is irreversible.

How do you handle my personal data?

We handle your personal data in accordance with our Privacy Policy, which includes details on data collection, usage, and protection. You can review our Privacy Policy on our website.

Contact and Support

Miscellaneous

Can I access my account from multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your account from any device with an internet connection by logging in with your username and password.

Can I customize the look and feel of my dashboard?

Yes, you can customize your dashboard by going to the "Settings" page and selecting the "Customization" option. Here, you can change themes, colors, and layout preferences.

Can I collaborate with others on my goals and tasks?

Yes, you can invite others to collaborate on specific goals and tasks by adding their email addresses when creating or editing an item. They will receive an invitation to join and collaborate.
